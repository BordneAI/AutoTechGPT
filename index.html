AutoTechGPT is an AI-powered assistant built for the auto industry, helping with diagnostics, repairs, training, and more. Get actionable answers and career advice—fast.

✨ Navigating the Menu System

As soon as you open the chat, select one of the main options or type a custom question:

  • 1️⃣ Diagnostics & Troubleshooting – Identify issues and get repair guidance.
  • 2️⃣ Maintenance & Service Intervals – View factory service schedules.
  • 3️⃣ OBD-II Code Lookup – Decode codes, causes, and fixes.
  • 4️⃣ Parts & Repair Info – Compare parts, torque specs, and tools.
  • 5️⃣ Technician Chat – Ask general repair questions or discuss techniques.
  • 6️⃣ Automotive Education – Access mechanic training & racing career advice.

Tip: Type exit menu or back to navigate out, or include your vehicle’s make/model/year for best results.

💡 Cool Things You Can Ask

  • "OBD-II code P0420 on 2017 Civic – what's wrong and how do I fix it?"
  • "Maintenance schedule for 2019 Toyota Camry Hybrid"

  • "Compare OEM vs aftermarket brake pads for Honda Accord"

  • "How to become ASE certified"
  • "Launch advanced electrical troubleshooting training"
  • "I added a downpipe to my Civic Si—will I get a CEL?"
  • "No emojis" (for strictly technical responses)

🚦 Advanced & Brand-Specific Features

  • Brand Support: Tailored advice for Honda, Toyota, Nissan, Dodge, Chevy, Ford, and more.
  • Performance Mode: Detects mods and racing queries, adding tuning tips & warranty cautions.
  • Education Tracks: Beginner to advanced diagnostics, plus motorsport career guidance.
  • Emoji Layer: Toggle off with no emojis for pure technical chat.
  • Safety & Ethics: No illegal or unsafe modification advice—always encourages OEM standards.
